🍲 Meal options

Each day, choose one breakfast, one snack (morning), one lunch, one snack (afternoon), and one dinner from the lists below.

Breakfast — choose 1
Option 1
Huevos al albañil (bricklayer-style eggs)
  • 1 whole egg
  • 5 egg whites
  • 3 slices turkey breast
  • 4 nopal tortillas
  • Salsa
  • 1 tomato, ¼ onion, garlic to taste, serrano and árbol chile to taste
  • Roast tomato, onion, and chiles; blend for salsa
Option 2
Fruit bowl + yogurt
  • 1 cup melon or papaya
  • 1 cup mixed berries or strawberries
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup granola or oats
  • 2 tbsp agave syrup
  • Yogurt dressing
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tbsp cream cheese, 1 tbsp honey
Option 3
Chilaquiles with chicken or eggs
  • 3 nopal tostadas or salmas
  • Chicken breast or 4 scrambled eggs
  • 140 g shredded chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp light cream (optional)
  • ½ cup diced tomato
  • 1 cup chopped lettuce
  • Onion to taste
  • ¼ avocado
  • Red or green salsa to taste
Option 4
Chicken sandwich with yogurt dressing
  • 2 slices whole wheat or rye bread
  • 160 g chicken breast
  • ½ cup diced tomato
  • Onion to taste
  • Yogurt dressing (same as option 2)
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt, 1 tbsp cream cheese, 1 tbsp honey
Snack — choose 1 (same list for both snack times)
Option 1
Blueberries & almonds in dark chocolate
  • 20 g dark chocolate, melted
  • 10 almonds
  • 10 blueberries
  • Coat berries and almonds in melted chocolate
Option 2
Berry mix with nuts
  • 10 cashews
  • 2 cups mixed berries
  • 10 almonds
Option 3
Strawberries with low-fat cream
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 4 tbsp low-fat cream
  • 2 tbsp shredded coconut
Option 4
Watermelon with dark chocolate
  • 2 cups diced watermelon
  • 30 g dark chocolate (≥70% cacao)
Lunch — choose 1
Option 1
Fish fillet with vegetables
  • 180 g grouper or sea bass fillet
  • ¼ avocado
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • ½ cup red onion
  • 1 cup boiled white rice
  • Lemon and dressing to taste
Option 2
Beef steak or chicken breast with salad
  • 180 g beef (round, sirloin tip, or similar) or grilled chicken breast
  • ¼ avocado
  • Green salad: 2 cups spinach, 1 cup lettuce
  • 1 tomato, diced
Option 3
Skirt steak or beef fillet with mashed potato
  • 160 g skirt steak or beef fillet
  • 1 cup mashed potato
  • ½ avocado
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tomato — optional low-sodium seasoning or salsa of choice
Option 4
Tuna bowl with rice
  • 2 cans tuna in water
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • ¼ avocado
  • 70 g mixed garden salad
Dinner — choose 1
Option 1
Grilled turkey rolls with panela & salad
  • 140 g turkey breast
  • 30 g panela cheese
  • 2 cups chopped spinach
  • ½ cup tomato
  • ½ cup mushrooms
  • 2 cups lettuce
Option 2
Tuna bowl
  • 1 can tuna in water
  • ¼ avocado
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 cup spinach or 5 asparagus spears
  • 1 package salmas
Option 3
Chicken “alambre” style
  • 160 g chicken
  • 1 cup bell peppers
  • ¼ cup red onion
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 5 asparagus spears
  • ¼ avocado
Option 4
Skirt steak salad
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 50 g corn kernels
  • 2 cups lettuce
  • ¼ avocado
